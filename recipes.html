Lagman

Lagman is a traditional Central Asian dish made with hand-pulled noodles, vegetables, and meat. It is known for its rich flavors and hearty ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • 500g beef or lamb, cut into small pieces
  • 200g hand-pulled noodles
  • 2 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 bell peppers, chopped
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 potato, diced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps:

  1. In a large pot, heat some oil and brown the meat until it is cooked through. Remove the meat and set aside.
  2. In the same pot, add the onions and garlic, and sauté until they are soft and fragrant.
  3. Add the tomatoes, bell peppers, carrots, and potatoes to the pot. Cook for a few minutes until the vegetables start to soften.
  4. Return the meat to the pot and add cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.
  5. Add enough water to cover the ingredients and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and let it simmer for about 30-40 minutes until the meat is tender and the flavors are well combined.
  6. While the stew is simmering, cook the hand-pulled noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  7. To serve, place a portion of noodles in a bowl and ladle the stew over them. Garnish with fresh cilantro.

Enjoy your delicious Lagman!

Plov

Plov, also known as pilaf, is a popular dish in Central Asia made with rice, meat, and vegetables. It is a flavorful and aromatic meal that is often served during special occasions.

Ingredients:

  • 500g lamb or beef, cut into small pieces
  • 2 cups long-grain rice
  • 2 large carrots, julienned
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, whole
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon coriander
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil for cooking
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Steps:

  1. Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Soak the rice in water for about 30 minutes, then drain.
  2. In a large pot or deep skillet, heat some oil and brown the meat until it is cooked through. Remove the meat and set aside.
  3. In the same pot, add the onions and sauté until they are golden brown.
  4. Add the carrots and cook for a few minutes until they start to soften.
  5. Return the meat to the pot and add cumin, coriander, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine.
  6. Add enough water to cover the ingredients and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and let it simmer for about 20 minutes.
  7. Add the drained rice on top of the meat and vegetables. Do not stir. Add whole garlic cloves on top.
  8. Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid and cook on low heat for about 20-25 minutes until the rice is cooked and has absorbed all the liquid.
  9. Once done, gently fluff the rice with a fork and mix everything together.
  10. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

The plov is done!

Caezar Salad

Caesar Salad is a classic salad known for its creamy dressing, crunchy croutons, and fresh romaine lettuce. It is a popular choice for a light and refreshing meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 head romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Caesar dressing
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup croutons
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges (optional)

Steps:

  1. Wash and chop the romaine lettuce into bite-sized pieces. Dry thoroughly using a salad spinner or paper towels.
  2. In a large salad bowl, combine the chopped lettuce and Caesar dressing. Toss well to ensure all the lettuce is coated with the dressing.
  3. Add the grated Parmesan cheese and croutons to the salad. Toss gently to combine.
  4. If desired, squeeze fresh lemon juice over the salad for an extra burst of flavor.
  5. Serve immediately as a side dish or add grilled chicken or shrimp for a complete meal.

Enjoy your delicious Caesar Salad!
